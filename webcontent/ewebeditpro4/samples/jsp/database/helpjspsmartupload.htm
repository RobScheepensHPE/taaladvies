We have integrated JSPSMARTUPLOAD. You will need to download the FREE servlet from www.jspsmart.com

From the jspsmart hompage:
1) Click the JSPSMARTUPLOAD button in the left navigation.
2) Click on Free Download or purchase it, if you require support.
3) Follow the instruction on their web page.
4) Download the zip file (jspSmartUpload.zip).
5) Unzip anywhere on you PC, but maintain the file structure.
6) The only files from the zip that you will need, are files located in the Web-inf directory. Copy the files located in jspsmartupload\Web-inf\classes into your classes directory on the server. For example with JRUN, Copy the files located in jspsmartupload\Web-inf\classes into C:\JRun\jsm-default\classes
7) Review these help files that are included in the jspSmartUpload.zip, for specific information, requirements, and setup regarding your web server:

  • jspSmartUpload/help/jspSmartUpload-InpriseAS-En.htm
  • jspSmartUpload-iPlanet-En.htm
  • jspSmartUpload-Jakarta-En.htm
  • jspSmartUpload-JRun-IIS-En.htm
  • jspSmartUpload-jRun-linux-En.htm
  • jspSmartUpload-Orion-En.htm
  • jspSmartUpload-Resin-En.htm
  • jspSmartUpload-ServletExec-En.htm
