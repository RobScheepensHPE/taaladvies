Polygon

Standard Toolbar Button

Description

Draws a polygon (that is, a closed figure surrounded by straight lines).

To use this command, follow these steps.

  1. Click the polygon command.
  2. Drag the line in one direction as far as you want.
  3. Drag the line in the other directions to complete the polygon.
  4. Double click the mouse to terminate the polygon.
  5. To change the polygon's line size or color, place the cursor on the line and press the right mouse button to display the Attributes menu.
