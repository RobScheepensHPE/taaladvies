Specifying a Search Direction

The search begins where the cursor is when you click Find Next. To make sure you locate every occurrence of a term, place the cursor at the top of the content before beginning the search.

If you begin the search from somewhere other than the top of the page, use the Direction field to search from the current location to the top or bottom of the file.
