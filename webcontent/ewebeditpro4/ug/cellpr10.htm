Working with Table Cells

Spanning More than One Row or Column

To have a table cell span more than one row or column, follow these steps.

  1. Place the cursor in the cell that will span rows or columns.
  2. Right click the mouse.
  3. Click Cell Properties from the menu.

  1. The Cell Properties dialog box appears. In the Rows Spanned or Columns Spanned field, enter the number of rows or columns that you want this cell to span.

  1. Click OK.
Effect of Spanning a Cell

When you set a cell to span rows or columns, the editor does not remove the cells that are in the way. Instead, it moves those cells across or down to the next available position.
