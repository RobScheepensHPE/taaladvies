Manipulating Your Table's Format

This section explains how to manipulate your table's format by specifying

  • a number of rows and columns
  • a width
  • horizontal alignment
  • a background color or image
  • border color and size

Note

You can also apply most of these properties to individual cells within a table. See Working with Table Cells for details.
