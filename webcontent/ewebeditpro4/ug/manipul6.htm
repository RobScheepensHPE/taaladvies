Manipulating Your Table's Format

Specifying Horizontal Alignment

You can specify your table's horizontal alignment (left, right, or center) within the browser.

Alignment
Example
left
right
center


If you specify right or left justify, you can wrap text around the table. To do this, move the cursor to the right or left of the table and begin typing.


Specify the table alignment at the Horizontal Alignment field on the Layout area of the Insert Table dialog box.
