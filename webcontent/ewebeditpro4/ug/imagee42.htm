Editing Images

Specifying Color Depth

To specify an image's color depth (that is, the number of colors available to an image), specify a bit depth. The color depth is derived from the bit depth. Here are the bit depth values.
Bit depth
Color depth
1
2 colors
4
16 colors
8
256 colors
24
16M colors
